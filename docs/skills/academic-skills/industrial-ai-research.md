# Industrial AI Research

Industrial AI literature research with mandatory intake questions, venue-aware source prioritization, and structured report outputs.

## When to use it

- surveying Industrial AI literature across venues
- comparing papers across conferences and journals
- identifying research gaps in predictive maintenance, scheduling, anomaly detection, or smart manufacturing

## Workflow

1. answer intake questions to define scope and objectives
2. build a search plan with venue-aware prioritization
3. collect and verify sources
4. synthesize findings across papers
5. assemble the final report in the chosen deliverable mode

## Main assets

- deliverable modes: research-brief, literature-map, venue-ranked survey, research-gap memo

## Notes

- Never fabricate paper metadata.
- Never present preprints as peer-reviewed publications.
